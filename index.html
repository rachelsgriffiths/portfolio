Hello! I’m Rachel, a web designer and small business owner passionate about helping other small businesses enhance their online presence through impactful websites.

Before diving into the world of design, I was a researcher and a self-proclaimed data nerd. My work now revolves more around graphics than numbers, but my evidence-based approach remains at the heart of what I do. Effective websites aren't just about looking good—it’s about what actually works.

A complete website redesign for content creator Harry's Advantures

Design | Web Development | Portfolio

The problem:

Originally designed as a personal blog, Harry's previous website didn’t align with his professional goals and was not attracting potential clients for his freelance services.

The solution:

Figma Prototype website design for countryside escape the Apple Shed

Design | Web Development | Portfolio

The problem:

The Apple Shed, an established countryside accommodation listed on Airbnb, could reduce their reliance on third-party booking platforms and avoid the associated commission fees with a website. The existing reliance on third-party listings limits their ability to fully control the booking process and maximise revenue.

The solution:

  • I designed a new website prototype using Figma, focusing on highlighting the key benefits and features of the countryside accommodation.
  • I prioritised an engaging user experience with rich visuals and compelling content to capture the essence of staying at the property.
  • Integrated a booking system directly into the prototype, giving owners full control over reservations and offering guests a more direct and personalised booking experience.
